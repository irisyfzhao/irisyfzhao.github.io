Project One

Increasing campus event attendance for student organizations.

My Roles

I was the sole UX designer in our 6-person team. And the rest 5 teammates were all from computer science major and responsible for the final development of the project. In addition, I also worked as the project manager, tracking the design process, and arranging meetings for our team and with clients.

Design Goal

Launching the platform with focus on promoting Chinese student organizations and their events.

Research

Competitive Analysis

img credit: Krisztina Szerovay

  • Building an individual platform is more effective than building a plugin on other platforms.

  • WeChat is designed for creating small but secure friend circle, thus its nature limits the efficient spreading of event information.

  • Facebook is only used by only a few Chinese students for language and user habits.
